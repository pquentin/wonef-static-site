WoNeF

WoNeF · WordNet du Français

We translated WordNet 3.0 to French. Every WordNet synset is filled with french literals (nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs). WoNeF is free as in free speech.

Try WoNeF

Adapted

Three versions are available : a high-precision version, a high F-score version and a high-coverage version.

Free

WoNeF is available under the CC-BY SA 3.0 FR license.

Evaluated

The three versions have been evaluated, the most precise version reaching 93% precision. The gold-standard used for evaluation is freely available.

Available

You can browse WoNeF on this website, but you can also download it as an XML file. The standard DEBVisDIC format maximizes its usefulness.
